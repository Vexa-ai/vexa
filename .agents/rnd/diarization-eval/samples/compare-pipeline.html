Top (__vexaTeamsAudioData capture) and bottom (③ SpeakerStreamManager → Whisper → publish) are identical in both. Main outsources who+when to Teams captions; the design in consideration produces who+when itself — segmentation owns the cut, diarization-on-turn owns the name (held until stable, trailing-held so the next speaker can't bleed into the previous tail), then feeds the frozen ③.
