Knowledge workers drown in meetings and email, and their actual knowledge — who said what, what was
decided, what's owed — is scattered across inboxes, docs, and memory. **Vexa gives each person an
agentic terminal**: a chat-first workbench whose backend is **meeting bots + an agentic runtime**, that
captures, organizes, and acts on their working life.

- **Claude Code × Outlook** — the agentic depth of a coding agent, over the surface area of email,
  calendar, meetings, and tasks.
- **The workspace is the memory** — a per-person git folder the agent reads and writes (see
  [Workspace](/primitives/workspace)).
- **Self-hostable & air-gapped** — runs in a customer VPC with BYO inference and zero egress; the finance
  verticals (DTCC / banks) are air-gapped by default.
