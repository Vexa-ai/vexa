---
title: "Live meetings"
description: "A dispatch the transcript stream keeps alive, emitting cards in real time."
---

The differentiator. During a call, a [dispatch](/architecture/dispatch) is kept **non-idle** by the live
[transcript](/capabilities/meetings) stream (TTL refreshed each beat). Behind a cheap salience gate it
emits [proactive cards](/architecture/governance) — new person, action item, decision — on its
[Stream](/primitives/stream); approving a card commits to the [workspace](/primitives/workspace) through the
same trusted-apply path. Not special — a `transcription`-triggered unit.
