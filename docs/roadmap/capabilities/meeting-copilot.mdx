---
title: "Meeting copilot"
description: "Real-time and post-meeting intelligence on top of the live transcript."
---

A [meeting](/core/meetings) produces a live transcript, so an [agent](/core/agents) can ride it:

- **During the call** — a dispatch the transcript keeps non-idle surfaces proactive cards (a new person,
  an action item, a decision); approving a card commits it to the [workspace](/concepts#workspace).
- **After the call** — a dispatch writes the notes, decisions, and follow-ups, and links them to the
  people and companies involved.

It's the same agent and the same governance as everything else — only the trigger (the transcript stream)
is different.
