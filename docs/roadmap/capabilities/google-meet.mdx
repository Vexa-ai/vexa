---
title: "Google Meet"
description: "Vexa joins a Google Meet as a bot and transcribes it in real time."
---

Vexa joins a Google Meet as a browser-container [bot](/primitives/runtime), captures the audio, and
produces a real-time `transcript.v1` stream that compiles into the [workspace](/primitives/workspace).
The bot attends like any participant — no plugins, no host configuration.

See [Meetings](/capabilities/meetings) for how the transcript becomes knowledge, and
[Live meetings](/capabilities/live-meetings) for acting on it during the call.
