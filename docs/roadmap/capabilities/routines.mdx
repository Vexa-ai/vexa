---
title: "Routines & automations"
description: "Entries in the scheduler — time or event triggered."
---

A **routine** is an entry in the [Scheduler](/primitives/scheduler) — a `(trigger, entry-point)` the user
manages. "After a meeting → update the workspace", "check news → record items", "every weekday 8am → brief"
are all routines, differing only by trigger (a schedule entry or an [Integration](/primitives/integration)
event). The durable schedule lives in redis, not in files.
