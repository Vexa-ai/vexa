---
title: "Chat"
description: "A now-dispatch over your workspace, streamed back."
---

Chat is a **`now` [dispatch](/architecture/dispatch)**: the message is the entry point, your
[workspace](/primitives/workspace) is mounted `rw` (chat is **trusted** input), and the reply
[streams](/primitives/stream) live. The same warm container serves the next turn (TTL keeps it until idle);
continuity is the session file. No special path — chat is just the unit with a message trigger.
